<p><p>Thermal conductive grease is used to enhance heat transfer between surfaces, such as in electronics or machinery. The market is divided into silicone-based and non-silicone-based grease. Silicone-based grease offers excellent thermal conductivity, stability across a wide temperature range, and electrical insulation, making it ideal for electronics. Non-silicone-based grease, often made from hydrocarbons or ceramics, provides high thermal performance without silicone's potential for outgassing, making it suitable for sensitive applications like aerospace or medical devices. Both types cater to specific thermal management needs.</p></p>
