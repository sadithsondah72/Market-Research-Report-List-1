<p><p>The Bale Net Wrap market includes two primary types: Knitted Net Wrap and Extruded Net Wrap. Extruded Net Wrap is produced by melting and shaping plastic into a mesh, providing high strength and resistance to tearing. Both types enhance baling efficiency, protect forage quality, and reduce storage losses, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
