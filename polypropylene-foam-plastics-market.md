<p><p>The Polypropylene Foam Plastics market is primarily categorized into two types: Extruded Polypropylene Foam Plastics and Expanded Polypropylene Foam Plastics. Extruded Polypropylene Foam is produced by forcing molten polypropylene through a die, resulting in a uniform foam structure ideal for applications requiring consistent thickness and density. In contrast, Expanded Polypropylene Foam is created through a process that involves expanding polypropylene beads, yielding a lightweight, resilient material often used in packaging, automotive, and insulation applications due to its superior cushioning properties.</p></p>
