<p><p>The pressure cooker market can be divided into two primary types: ordinary energy pressure cookers and electric pressure cookers. In contrast, electric pressure cookers are self-contained units that utilize an electric heating element, allowing for precise temperature control and programmable cooking options. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, enhancing convenience and efficiency in the kitchen.</p></p>
