<p><p>The flat automobile horn market is primarily categorized into two types: electronic horns and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns utilize sound-producing circuits and amplifiers to generate a wide range of tones, offering versatility and higher sound clarity. In contrast, electromagnetic horns rely on a mechanical diaphragm and a coil system to create sound, often producing a classic, robust tone. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, vehicle designs, and regulatory standards, impacting their application in modern automotive technology.</p></p>
