<p><p>The solar inverter market is categorized into three main types: Central Solar Inverters, String Solar Inverters, and Micro Solar Inverters. Central Solar Inverters are designed for large-scale installations, converting power from multiple solar panels into a single output. String Solar Inverters connect several panels in series, making them suitable for residential and commercial systems. Micro Solar Inverters, mounted on individual panels, optimize output and enhance performance, especially in shaded areas. Each type serves distinct application needs in harnessing solar energy efficiently.</p></p>
