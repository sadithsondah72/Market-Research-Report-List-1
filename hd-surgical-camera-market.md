<p><p>The HD surgical camera market features two primary types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are known for their faster processing speeds, lower power consumption, and compact designs, making them ideal for high-definition applications in surgical settings. In contrast, CCD cameras offer superior image quality and sensitivity, particularly in low-light environments, which is crucial for detailed surgical procedures. Both types are integral to advancing surgical imaging technology, enhancing visual clarity and precision during operations.</p></p>
