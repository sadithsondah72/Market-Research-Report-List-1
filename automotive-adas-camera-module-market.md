<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive ADAS Camera Module market primarily features two types of imaging technology: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) cameras. CCD cameras offer high-quality images with superior light sensitivity, making them suitable for low-light conditions, but tend to be bulkier and consume more power. In contrast, CMOS cameras are smaller, lighter, and more power-efficient, facilitating real-time processing and advanced features. As ADAS technologies evolve, CMOS cameras are gaining prominence due to their versatility and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
