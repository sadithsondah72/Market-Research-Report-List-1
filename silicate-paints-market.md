<p><p>Silicate paints are mineral-based coatings known for durability and eco-friendliness. **Pure Silicate Paint** uses potassium silicate as a binder, offering excellent adhesion and weather resistance, ideal for masonry and mineral surfaces. **Sol-Silicate Paint** combines silicate with silica sol, enhancing flexibility and ease of application, suitable for both new and old substrates. **Other** types include hybrid formulations with organic additives, balancing performance and versatility for diverse applications. These paints are valued for breathability, longevity, and resistance to mold and UV degradation.</p></p>
