<p><p>The PC halogen-free flame retardant market is divided into two primary types: organic and inorganic flame retardants. Organic flame retardants typically utilize phosphorus, nitrogen, or intumescent systems to enhance thermal stability and reduce flammability. In contrast, inorganic flame retardants often incorporate compounds like metal hydroxides or phosphates, which are effective in delaying ignition and combustion. Both types are essential for meeting regulatory standards and improving safety in applications such as electronics, automotive, and construction materials.</p></p>
