<p><ul><li>Shaped High-duty Refractory</li><li>Unshaped High-duty Refractory</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-duty refractory material market is divided into two main types: shaped and unshaped high-duty refractories. Shaped high-duty refractories are pre-formed materials, like bricks and tiles, designed for specific applications and configurations in high-temperature environments. Conversely, unshaped high-duty refractories are made from raw materials that can be molded into any shape, like castables and rammables, allowing for greater versatility in applications. Both types are essential for industries such as metal, glass, and ceramics, where thermal resistance is crucial.</p></p>
