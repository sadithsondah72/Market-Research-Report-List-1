<p><p>Natural Language Processing (NLP) market types include rule-based, statistical, and hybrid approaches. Rule-based NLP relies on predefined linguistic rules and lexicons to process language, offering precision but limited adaptability. Statistical NLP utilizes algorithms and data-driven models to analyze large datasets, enabling flexibility and generalization but requiring significant training data. Hybrid approaches combine the strengths of both methods, leveraging rule-based structures for accuracy while employing statistical techniques for scalability, thus providing a balanced solution for various NLP applications.</p></p>
