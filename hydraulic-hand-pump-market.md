<p><strong>The Hydraulic Hand Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump</li><li>Double Acting Hydraulic Hand Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydraulic hand pump market consists primarily of two types: single acting and double acting hydraulic hand pumps. Single acting pumps deliver fluid on one stroke of the handle, using a return spring to retract, making them simpler and more cost-effective for lighter applications. In contrast, double acting pumps operate with fluid output on both strokes of the handle, providing greater efficiency and higher pressure capabilities, making them ideal for more demanding industrial tasks and applications requiring continuous flow.</p></p>
