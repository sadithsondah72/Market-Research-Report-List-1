<p><strong>The Handheld Breath Alcohol Analyser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Sample</li><li>Passive Sample</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld breath alcohol analyser market can be categorized into two main types: direct sample and passive sample analyzers. Direct sample analyzers require individuals to blow into the device, providing precise BAC (blood alcohol concentration) readings. These are commonly used by law enforcement and personal users. In contrast, passive sample analyzers can detect alcohol vapors in the air without direct interaction, making them useful for screening groups of people, such as at events or in workplaces.</p></p>
