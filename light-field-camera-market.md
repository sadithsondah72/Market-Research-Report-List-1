<p><ul><li>Standard Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Focused Plenoptic Camera</li><li>Coded Aperture Camera</li><li>Stereo With Plenoptic Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The light field camera market comprises several types, each leveraging unique technologies. Standard plenoptic cameras capture light fields using microlens arrays, enabling refocusing after the shot. Focused plenoptic cameras enhance depth resolution by optimizing imaging settings for specific focus distances. Coded aperture cameras utilize coded patterns to gather depth information efficiently. Stereo light field cameras combine two perspectives to improve 3D imaging capabilities. These innovations cater to various applications in photography, virtual reality, and computer vision, expanding capabilities in capturing and processing light fields.</p></p>
