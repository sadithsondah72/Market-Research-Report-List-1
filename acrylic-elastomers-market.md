<p><strong>The Acrylic Elastomers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Acrylic Co-monomer Elastomer (ACM)</li><li>Ethylene Acrylic Elastomers (AEM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Acrylic elastomers are synthetic rubbers known for their heat, oil, and weather resistance. **Acrylic Co-monomer Elastomer (ACM)** is a type of acrylic elastomer made by copolymerizing acrylic esters with other monomers, offering excellent oil and heat resistance, making it ideal for automotive seals and gaskets. **Ethylene Acrylic Elastomers (AEM)** are copolymers of ethylene and acrylic esters, providing superior heat, ozone, and chemical resistance, commonly used in automotive and industrial applications like hoses and belts. Both types are valued for durability in harsh environments.</p></p>
