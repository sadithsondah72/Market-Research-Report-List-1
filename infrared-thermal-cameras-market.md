<p><strong>The Infrared Thermal Cameras Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Short Wave Infrared</li><li>Mid-Wave Infrared</li><li>Long Wave Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared thermal camera market is segmented into three types based on wavelength: Short Wave Infrared (SWIR), Mid-Wave Infrared (MWIR), and Long Wave Infrared (LWIR). SWIR cameras operate between 0.9 to 1.7 micrometers, ideal for industrial applications and monitoring moisture content. MWIR cameras, ranging from 3 to 5 micrometers, are commonly used in military and gas detection applications. LWIR cameras, operating between 8 to 14 micrometers, excel in thermal imaging for building inspections, surveillance, and firefighting due to their ability to detect heat signatures effectively.</p></p>
