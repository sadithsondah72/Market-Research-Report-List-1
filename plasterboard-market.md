<p><h1>Insights into Plasterboard Market Size: Analysing Market Share, Trends, and Growth from 2025 to 2032</h1></p><p><strong>Plasterboard Market Analysis and Latest Trends</strong></p>
<p><p>Plasterboard, also known as drywall or gypsum board, is a panel made of calcium sulfate dihydrate (gypsum) sandwiched between two thick sheets of paper. It is widely used in construction for creating walls, ceilings, and partitions due to its lightweight, fire-resistant, and soundproofing properties. Plasterboard is easy to install, cost-effective, and offers a smooth finish, making it a preferred material in residential, commercial, and industrial construction projects.</p><p>The Plasterboard Market is experiencing steady growth, driven by increasing construction activities, urbanization, and the demand for sustainable building materials. The market is expected to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 5.4% during the forecast period. Key factors contributing to this growth include the rise in infrastructure development, renovation projects, and the adoption of energy-efficient and eco-friendly construction practices. Emerging economies are witnessing significant demand due to rapid urbanization and industrialization.</p><p>Recent trends in the Plasterboard Market include the development of advanced products such as moisture-resistant, fire-rated, and acoustic plasterboards to meet specific construction needs. Additionally, manufacturers are focusing on lightweight and recyclable materials to align with sustainability goals. The integration of digital tools for precision cutting and installation is also gaining traction, enhancing efficiency in construction processes. Overall, the market is poised for expansion, supported by innovation and increasing demand for modern construction solutions.</p></p>
