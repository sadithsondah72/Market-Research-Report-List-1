<p><p>The asphalt additives market includes various types, each serving specific purposes. Asphalt modifiers enhance performance characteristics, improving durability and temperature resilience. Antistripping agents prevent moisture damage, enhancing adhesion to aggregates. Asphalt emulsifiers facilitate the mixing of asphalt with water for easier application. Surfactant additives improve flow and stability. Foam stabilizers are used in warm mix asphalt to maintain consistency during storage and application. Other additives may include recycled materials or specialty chemicals tailored for specific projects, optimizing asphalt properties.</p></p>
