<p><p>The Check Engine Light market can be divided into two main types: intermittent and continuous. An intermittent Check Engine Light occurs sporadically, often triggered by temporary issues like loose gas caps or minor sensor faults, which may resolve themselves. In contrast, a continuous Check Engine Light signifies consistent, ongoing problems, such as severe engine malfunctions or significant system failures requiring immediate attention. Both types drive consumer behavior toward diagnostics and repair services, influencing market dynamics and service offerings.</p></p>
