<p><p>The Blister Heat Seal Coating market is segmented into two main types: solvent-based and water-based coatings. Solvent-based blister heat seal coatings use organic solvents for formulation, offering strong adhesion and durability, making them suitable for various packaging applications. In contrast, water-based blister heat seal coatings utilize water as a solvent, providing a more environmentally friendly option with lower volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. Both types serve to create effective seals for blister packs, ensuring product protection and shelf life.</p></p>
