<p><p>The skin moisturizers market encompasses various types, including Natural Moisturizing Factors (NMF), Polyhydric Alcohol Humectants, and Amino Acids and Polypeptides. NMFs, like urea and lactic acid, mimic skin's natural hydration components. Polyhydric alcohol humectants, such as glycerin and propylene glycol, draw moisture from the environment to the skin. Amino acids and polypeptides, essential for skin health, aid in hydration, barrier function, and collagen synthesis. Together, these ingredients enhance skin hydration, elasticity, and overall appearance, driving consumer interest and market growth.</p></p>
