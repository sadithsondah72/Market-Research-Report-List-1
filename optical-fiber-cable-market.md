<p><strong>The Optical Fiber Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical fiber cable market primarily consists of two types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber features a larger diameter core, allowing multiple light modes to propagate, making it suited for short-distance communication, such as within buildings. In contrast, single-mode fiber has a smaller core, enabling only one mode of light to travel, which enhances performance over long distances, making it ideal for telecommunications and data networks. These distinct characteristics cater to varying connectivity needs in diverse applications.</p></p>
